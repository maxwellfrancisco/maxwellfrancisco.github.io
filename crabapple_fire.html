Wildfire Burn Severity of the 2025 Crabapple Fire near Fredericksburg, TX

Tools: ArcGIS Pro, Raster Algebra

Overview

The Crabapple Fire was located in Gillespie county, roughly 10 miles north of Fredericksburg near Enchanted Rock. It began on March 15, 2025 before being declared 100% contained by the City of Fredericksburg on March 21.

Data

  • Sentinel L2A - 20m^2 resolution

Methods

  • 6 images from March 14, 19, 21, 24, 29, and April 8.
  • Calculate Normalized Burn Ratio (NBR) with raster algebra NIR - SWIR / NIR + SWIR.
  • Calculate Difference Normalized Burn Ratio (dBNR) Pre-fire NBR - Post-fire NBR for each image after the start date.
  • Clipping of raster to the area of interest.
  • Reclassification of raster into 4 classes, NoData for values below 0.1 and 3 severity classes above.

Limitations

  • The severity classes used are more adjusted for heavily forested environments, not the lower values of a grassland fire. Another scale adapted for grasslands would work better to measure burn severity in this area.
